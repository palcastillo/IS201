About Me

My name is Paloma Castillo and I am currently in my sophomore year at BYU. I was born and raised in Norfolk, Nebraska from parents who immigrated to the U.S from Mexico. I am a first generation student studying pre-business applying to the Marketing program. I am 20 years old and the youngest of four siblings. I completed my freshman year at BYU- Idaho and transferred to BYU Provo to complete the rest of my schooling. With the help of my father I was also able to grow up playing in a Mariachi Band. It helped me learn to love my Mexican culture. When I moved to Utah I was able to participate in a Mexican folk dance group. It was always a dream of mine to learn how to dance traditional dances from Mexico and I cherished each moment I was in the group. I am very grateful to have two parents that love and support everything I do.
